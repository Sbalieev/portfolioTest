We have been asked as a projectgroup (5) to develop an online campaign for the dutch tomato grower factory Looye Kwekers. The online campaign needs to connect Looye Kwekers with her target group. Looye has also a great goal to achieve. In 2025, 33 out of 1000 people inside the target group must buy at least 1 product of Looye every month. Setting this goal for herself, Looye wants to become the best selled and loved tomato grower in The Netherlands, Germany and Belgium.

Our solution was to create a way that not only Looye sells her products, achieves her goal and has a strong bonding with her target group. We also tried to find a way in order to fulfill the wishes and needs of the target group.

After some research we came to the conclusion that we had to design a way in order to help the target group to eat healthier and in a way that will fill up to their own lifestyle.

My biggest role in this project group was to do deskreseach and fieldresearch about the target group. Nevertheless to design some of the final products.
