In this project we worked as a team (5) and did a research based on a given casus. Thanks to the theoretical information we got from the book Seductive Interaction Design written by Stephen P. Anderson we were able to make a redesign for this given casus. Every process through this project has been documentated.

The casus for this project is about an online musicstore named Velvet Music. Velvet has a wide product range and thus sells products of different music genres. In order to get an optimalized product, we had to focus on a specific music genre. The delivered product contains a redesign of the homepage and detailpage based on the specific chosen music genre.

Online webshop of Velvet Music does not require the expectations and has to be redesigned. Our goal was to optimalise the store experience of customers. In order to achieve our goal we had to chose a certain target audience.

One of my main roles in our projectteam was to make sure the detailpage meets the requirements we set up as a team. The requirements are based on the 52 principle Mental Notes.
